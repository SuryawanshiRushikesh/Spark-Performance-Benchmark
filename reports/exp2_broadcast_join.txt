============================================================
EXPERIMENT 2 — Broadcast Join Optimization
============================================================

Built On:
- Parquet format (Exp1 applied)

Change Applied:
- Broadcast users (100k rows)
- Broadcast products (10k rows)

Control:
- Shuffle Partitions : 200
- No caching
- No partition pruning

------------------------------------------------------------
Stage Timings
------------------------------------------------------------

Stage           Exp1        Exp2
------------------------------------------------------------
Read            5.63s       4.97s
Join            2.34s       2.18s
Aggregation     3.10s       2.87s
Write           4.62s       4.24s
------------------------------------------------------------
TOTAL           15.69s      14.27s
------------------------------------------------------------

Observations:
- Join improved slightly (~7%).
- Overall runtime improved by ~1.4 seconds.
- Broadcast impact limited due to local execution mode.
- Join was not the primary bottleneck.

Conclusion:
Broadcast join provides modest gains at this data scale.
Optimization impact depends heavily on workload size and cluster setup.
